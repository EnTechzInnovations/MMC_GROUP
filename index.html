Who We Are

A Legacy Built on
Trust & Vision

MMC Group is one of Kerala's most respected diversified conglomerates, built on a foundation of integrity, excellence, and innovation. Since 2001, we have expanded from a single enterprise into a multi-sector powerhouse that touches millions of lives across India.

Our eight business verticals — from infrastructure to technology — share one common thread: an unwavering commitment to our stakeholders and the communities we serve. We grow not just in scale, but in trust.

Integrity First
Every decision anchored in honesty and ethical business practices that create lasting stakeholder trust.
Sustainable Growth
Building businesses that create lasting value for shareholders, employees, and the communities around us.
Innovation Driven
Embracing cutting-edge technology and modern practices to stay ahead in a rapidly transforming world.
